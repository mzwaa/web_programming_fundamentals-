Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This rich and hearty spaghetti Bolognese is a traditional Italian favorite made with ground beef, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. It's a perfect dish for family dinners and can be prepared in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan if desired.

Tips

For an even deeper flavor, simmer the sauce for longer—up to an hour if you have time. You can also add a splash of red wine when cooking the beef for a richer taste.

Nutrition

Per serving (without Parmesan): 520 calories, 18g fat, 60g carbohydrates, 28g protein.
